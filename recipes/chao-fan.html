chao fan recipe

Description

Not only is this fried rice a staple, it’s also versatile – easily adapted to almost any type of protein or meat-free preference.

Sub chicken for equal parts shrimp and ham, fry up some tofu beforehand, or leave it as it is

Ingredients

  • 1 cup White Rice
  • 2 cups chicken breast, cooked, diced to ½ size
  • 2 tsps dry sherry
  • 2 tsps cornstarch
  • 4 tbsps oil, divided
  • ½ cup onion, diced
  • ½ cup rib of celery, diced
  • soy sauce, to taste
  • 2 tbsps boiling water
  • ¼ cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 slice fresh ginger root
  • 1 each minced garlic
  • 3 eggs, lightly beaten

Steps

  1. Prepare rice according to package directions. For best results refrigerate cooked rice overnight.
  2. In a small bowl, sprinkle cooked chicken pieces with sherry and cornstarch. Heat 2 tablespoons oil in a wok or frying pan over high heat.
  3. In the same wok, add onion and celery, and stir-fry about 2-minutes, shaking pan to prevent browning. Add 1 tablespoon soy sauce and 1 to 2 tablespoons boiling water. Reduce heat to medium. Cover and steam 1-minute.
  4. Add mushrooms, cover and cook until tender 1 to 2-minutes. Remove vegetables and reserve. Reduce heat to medium and add 2 tablespoons oil. When hot, add ginger and garlic, and sauté. Discard when lightly browned. Add chicken, then toss and fry for 2 minutes.
  5. Add rice and cooked vegetables. Toss until hot. Add oil if needed. Make a well in center of mixture. Add eggs and stir until scrambled. Mix well, then season with soy sauce and serve immediately.
