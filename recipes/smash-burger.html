smash burger recipe

Description

Create a restaurant style Smash Burger at home in minutes and for a lot less money. You are going to love the crispy brown crust that forms as the burger cooks so quickly.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef. I use an 80/20 meant to fat ratio. One pound makes four burgers.
  • Seasoning. Use your favorite beef seasoning salt. I like Montreal Steak Seasoning.
  • Hamburger Buns. Butter and toast your buns before cooking burgers so they are ready as soon as the burgers are.
  • Hamburger Sauce.
  • Pickles. We like dill pickles.

Steps

  1. Form hamburger into four loosely shaped balls. Fresh ground beef will have more flavor than frozen meat.
  2. Heat Griddle. You want your griddle hot, about 375 degrees F. I like to put my cast iron griddle on my outdoor grill but you can also do it on your stovetop but I find it sets my smoke alarm off frequently! If you don’t have a griddle you can also use a cast iron pan.
  3. Place the balls of meat onto a hot griddle.
  4. After placing hamburger on hot griddle place a piece of parchment paper over the top and then smash. You want a nice thin patty. The parchment paper keeps the meat from sticking to the spatula.
  5. Add seasoning salt generously.
  6. Cook for 2-3 minutes, the burger cooks fast. The edges of the burger will be lacy and may even have some holes in it, this is what creates the crispy edges that are a trademark of the Smash Burger.
  7. Flip the burger by using a metal spatula to “scrap” the burger up and flip. You want to get all that crusty goodness that is forming on the other side of the burger as it cooks.
  8. Add cheese immediately after flipping.
  9. Put the sauce onto the bottom of the bun, then add the burger, and then the pickles. and then close the buns.
